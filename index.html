TWRP Recovery

 The teamwin recovery project (TWRP) is the most used custom recovery; with this custom recovery you will be able to flash roms, wipe partitions, backup them, and many other things to manage your device without booting into android
You can download my TWRP port for Galaxy A5 in the lineageos 15.1 releases website: HERE
TWRP website: twrp.me


Multirom

 Multirom is a mod for android device that allows them to boot multiple roms, both android-based roms, such as lineageos, and linux-based roms such as sailfishos and ubuntu touch.
Download my multirom port for Galaxy A5 here: HERE
To manage multirom roms you need Multirom's TWRP recovery. You can get it HERE
If you want to uninstall multirom, you can download the uninstaller HERE
You can find more info about multirom here: github.com/Tasssadar/multirom/wiki
